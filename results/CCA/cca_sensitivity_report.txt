========================================================================
 CROSS-SESSION PCA-CCA SENSITIVITY ANALYSIS: VIDEO VS IMU FEATURES 
========================================================================

1. DATASET CONFIGURATION
----------------------------------------
Strict Cross-Session Protocol (NO FLIPS)
Train Samples (FirstRun, SecondRun): 576
Test Samples (ThirdRun): 228
Original Video Dimensionality (Depth+RGB+IR): 73728 features
Original IMU Dimensionality: 4930 features

2. METHODOLOGY RATIONALE
----------------------------------------
To prevent artificial 1.0 correlations due to overfitting and the P >> N
problem, the dataset was strictly split temporally (Cross-Session).
PCA and CCA models were fitted strictly on the Training subset (Run 1 & 2).
The reported Canonical Correlation reflects the out-of-sample projection
on the unseen Test subset (Run 3), empirically measuring the true semantic gap.
